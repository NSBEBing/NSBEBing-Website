Thank You to Our Supporters

The National Society of Black Engineers Binghamton Chapter thrives thanks to the generous support of our corporate sponsors. These partnerships enable us to provide professional development opportunities, technical workshops, and community outreach programs that empower our members to excel in their engineering careers.
